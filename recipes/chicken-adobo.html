Famous Chicken Adobo

About this dish:

This chicken adobo recipe is simple to make and loved by all who try it! It has been modified to be a bit saucier than traditional adobo and is delicious served over rice.

Ingredients:

Here are the ingredients for FAMOUS Chicken Adobo!

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • ⅔ cup low sodium soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 bay leaf

How to Make FAMOUS Chicken Adobo (Steps):

  1. Brown the chicken:

    2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside.

  2. Onion base:

    3. Add onion and garlic to the skillet; cook until softened and brown, about 3 to 5 minutes.

  3. Get Soy'd Up:

    4. Pour in soy sauce and vinegar and season with garlic powder, black pepper, and bay leaf.

  4. Chicken Adobo Time:

    5. Return chicken to pan, increase heat to high, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until chicken is tender and cooked through, 35 to 40 minutes.

  5. Enjoy:

    6. It's time to enjoy your delious FAMOUS chicken adobo!
